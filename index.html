Lorelei - "Lorry"
Lorelei Alcidibias, often called 'Lorry', 20 years of age at the time of [insert inciting incident]. She was born to Emayn and Dives in the town of Vindkilde. She was born into a semi-aristocratic lifestyle, not having to worry about her next meal or the roof over her head. She always had a bit of adventure in her soul. This feeling was not recuperated by her home's lifestyle. But, at the age of 13, she heard that her older sister, Simile, was leaving to go on an adventure, Lorry needed to join her. She begged and pleaded with her older sister for 48 straight hours.
During this time, the sisters met lots of different creatures. For the first time in their lives, they met people of races other than elves or humans. They met dwarfs, half-lings, dragon-born and most interestingly: Tieflings. Lorry was fascinated by them. The first taste of adventure she got was the sight of tiefling, a woman named [Insert Name]. Lorry was utterly perplexed by the sight of a tiefling, but she was more intrigued than anything. She developed a liking for tieflings and their charisma, which still stands today.

College of Mesmerism

Bards of the college of Mesmerism master the arts of charm and deceit. They utilize the tools that they need to manipulate others to heed their words, utilizing these mind tricks usually for their own benefit. Bards of this college always have a plan for when these ploys are discovered.

College of Mesmerism Features

Bard Level Feature
3rd Mesmerism Trick, Hypnotic Stare
6th Towering ego
14th Ruler of Minds

Mesmerism Trick

When you join the College of Mesmerism at 3rd level, creatures charmed by one of your enchantment spells of 1st level or higher remain unaware that they are being charmed.

Hypnotic Stare

At 3rd you can use your bonus action and one bardic inspiration die to stare at a creature that you can see within 60ft that can see you. That creature has disadvantage in Wisdom savings throws against your spells for 1 minute or untill you stare at another creature, the creature dies, you and the creature are more than 60ft apart or you fall unconscious or die.

At the end of each of the creature's turns, the creature can make a Wisdom saving throw against your save DC. On a successful save, the effect ends.

Creatures under the effect of your stare don't know that they are being stared at or under the effect unless you allow it. Creatures tht can't be charmed are immune to this effect.

Towering Ego

at 6th level, you gain proficiency in Wisdom saving throws. If you already have this proficiency, you instead gain proficiency in Intelligence or Charisma saving throws.(You choose)

Ruler of Minds

at 14th level, you can use an action to force a humanoid creature under the effect of your Hypnotic Stare to make a Wisdom Saving throw. On a failed save, the creature a thrall(As per the Dominate Person spell) under your control for the next hour.The creature remains enthralled untill you or your allies attackor damage it, you successfully use this feature agian or you die.

Once the effect ends, or if the creature succeeds on its initial saving throw against this effect, you can't use this feature again untill you finish a long rest.
